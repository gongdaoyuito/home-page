八丁味噌

大豆と塩のみを原料に大きな木桶に仕込み、天然の川石を職人の手で山のように積み上げて重石とし、この八丁町（旧・八丁村）の気候風土のなかで二夏二冬（2年以上）天然醸造で熟成させて出来上がります。 味は大豆のうま味を凝縮した濃厚なコクと少々の酸味、渋味のある独特な風味が特徴です。

Hatcho miso

It is brewed in a large wooden vat using only soybeans and salt, then weighed down with a pile of natural river stones by craftsmen, and naturally brewed for two summers and two winters (over two years) in the climate of Haccho-machi (formerly Haccho Village). It is characterized by its unique flavor, which is rich and concentrated with the umami of soybeans, a little sourness, and astringency.

えびせんべい

じゃがいもなどから作られる澱粉と海老のすり身を混ぜて作る菓子のこと。米粉や小麦粉で作られる一般的な「せんべい」と違い、澱粉の膨化力を生かしたサクッとした軽い食感とえびの風味や旨味が魅力です。 愛知県の名産品として有名な「えびせんべい」の主な生産地域は三河地方と知多地方です。三河湾や伊勢湾で獲れる良質なえびを使用している商品が多いのも特徴です。

rice crackers seasoned with red pepper

It is a confectionery made by mixing starch made from potatoes and other ingredients with shrimp paste. Unlike ordinary rice crackers made of rice flour or wheat flour, the crispy, light texture and shrimp flavor and delicious taste are appealing, taking advantage of the swelling power of starch. Shrimp crackers, a famous specialty of Aichi Prefecture, are mainly produced in the Mikawa and Chita regions. Many products are characterized by the use of high-quality shrimp caught in Mikawa Bay and Ise Bay.
