名古屋城

織田信長誕生の城とされる今川氏築城の那古野城（なごやじょう）の跡周辺に、徳川家康が天下普請によって築城した。以降明治維新まで徳川御三家の筆頭とされる尾張徳川家17代の居城だった。 大阪城、熊本城とともに日本三名城に数えられ、伊勢音頭では「伊勢は津でもつ、津は伊勢でもつ、尾張名古屋は城でもつ」と詠われている。大天守に上げられた金鯱は城だけでなく名古屋の街の象徴である。本丸御殿は二条城の二の丸御殿と並ぶ武家風書院造の双璧と評価されていた。 大小天守と櫓や門、御殿などの一部は昭和戦前期まで残存していたが、1945年の名古屋大空襲で大部分を焼失した（西南隅櫓など6棟は焼失を免れて現存している）。戦後に天守などが鉄筋コンクリートで外観復元され、城跡は名城公園の南園として整備されている。

Nagoya Castle

Tokugawa Ieyasu built the castle by fuchin (building construction) around the ruins of Nagoya Castle, which was built by the Imagawa clan and is said to be the birthplace of Oda Nobunaga. Since then, the castle was the residence of the 17th generation of the Owari Tokugawa family, the first of the three Tokugawa families, until the Meiji Restoration. Along with Osaka Castle and Kumamoto Castle, it is one of the three most famous castles in Japan, and in the Ise Ondo (Japanese folk song), "Ise wa Tsu de motsu, Tsu wa Ise de motsu, Owari Nagoya wa shiro de motsu" is recited. The golden orcas on the main keep are a symbol not only of the castle but also of the city of Nagoya. The Honmaru Goten was regarded, along with the Ninomaru Goten of Nijo Castle, as the twin perfect examples of the samurai-style shoin-zukuri style. The large and small keepers, turrets, gates, and part of the palace survived until the early Showa period, but most of them were destroyed by fire in the Nagoya air raids of 1945 (six buildings, including the southwest corner turret, escaped destruction and are still standing). After the war, the castle tower and other structures were restored to their original appearance using reinforced concrete, and the ruins of the castle are now maintained as the South Park in Meijo Park.

レゴランド

レゴブロックが世界7ヵ国で展開する屋外型キッズテーマパークで、家族で楽しめるスポット。レゴブロックで遊び、レゴモデルを創作することができるのはもちろん、レゴブロックの世界観に思い切り浸り、楽しむことができる体験アトラクションがたくさんある。 レゴブロックが作られている工場を見学できたり、全てがレゴでできた街に入ってその街の住人になれたり。様々なアトラクションやお子さんが自分から進んで楽しむことができるアクティビティも数多くある。

Legoland

This is an outdoor kids' theme park that LEGO Bricks operates in seven countries around the world, and is a fun spot for the whole family. In addition to playing with LEGO bricks and creating LEGO models, there are many hands-on attractions where visitors can immerse themselves in the world of LEGO bricks and enjoy themselves to the fullest. You can visit the factory where LEGO bricks are made, enter a city made entirely of LEGO, and become a resident of that city. There are a variety of attractions and many activities that children can enjoy on their own initiative.
